## TC-01: Perform Parkour Movement Across Rooftops (Happy Path)

**Test Type:** Manual  
**Test Level:** Gameplay / Functional  
**Priority:** High  

### Preconditions
- Game launched successfully
- Player character spawned on a rooftop
- Parkour and movement systems enabled

### Steps
1. Move the character forward using standard movement controls
2. Perform a jump between two rooftops
3. Execute a parkour action (vault, climb, or ledge grab)
4. Continue traversing rooftops without falling off the level

### Expected Result
- Player movement feels responsive and stable
- Parkour actions trigger correctly
- Player remains within the intended playable area
- Gameplay flow is uninterrupted
